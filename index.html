King's Landing

Since 2011 Dubrovnik is used as setting for the most popular show ever. Seasons 2,3,4,5,6 and 7 were filmed all around the world.

All of our guides are licenced profesionals with experience on set and of course devoted ‘Game of Thrones’ fans.

Enjoy breathtaking locations in Dubrovnik and hear interesting facts about history of Dubrovnik (It's how others call King's landing), details behind scenes during the filming.

How did this ongoing phenomenon has efected locals and Dubrovnik tourism?

Did you know that George.R.Martin 's principal inspiration was English Wars of the Roses between the houses of Lancaster and York, which remind a lot of houses of Lannister and Stark?
